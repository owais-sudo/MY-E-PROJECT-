ABOUT US

About Cambridge Hospital: Cambridge Hospital is a well-established and trusted healthcare institution, serving the community since 1928. Known for its commitment to excellence in medical care, the hospital offers a wide range of services including Emergency, ICU, Surgery, Pediatrics, and Maternity care. Equipped with modern technology and a team of experienced healthcare professionals, Cambridge Hospital ensures quality treatment for all patients. In addition to healthcare services, the hospital also focuses on medical education by offering diploma programs in fields like Nursing and Laboratory Technology. Cambridge Hospital continues to uphold its mission of providing compassionate and reliable healthcare to everyone.


We have a team of experienced professionals, modern infrastructure, and a patient-centered approach to ensure you get the best care possible.
